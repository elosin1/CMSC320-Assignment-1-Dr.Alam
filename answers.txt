Q3: What is the difference between merging and rebasing?

Answer: When we rebase, our commits in a different branch are put on top of the most recent commit in the branch we are rebasing on. Merging on the other hand creates a merge commit and does not move the feature branch but instead connects it to the most recent commit of the main branch with the merge commit
